About finch

Vision and Mission

Vision

To become the default infrastructure for intentional early-career recruiting — replacing cold, volume-based application systems with a smarter, outcome-driven approach where students are evaluated more efficiently and the path from education to experience is transparent and merit-based.

Mission

To transform the internship application process from a frustrating numbers game into an intentional, data-driven strategy that helps students earn the opportunities they deserve — while Finch streamlines, optimizes, and strengthens their path to interviews.

Origin Story

Carlos was trying to apply to internships as a CS major and was not getting any responses from mass applications. He understood his own pain points and thought of a more efficient way to approach the process. He built a functioning backend that minimized the number of applications he had to fill out while maximizing his success rate for interviews. That's how Finch was born.
